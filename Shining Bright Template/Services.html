Our Services

Packages

We have a variety of packages beginning in 30, 60 and 90-minute increments to meet your needs. Don't see a package? Email us at shiningbrighttutors@gmail.com to schedule a call so that we may customize a package for you!

Elementary School Tutoring

Do you have a child in Elementary School (K-5) that needs additional support in class? We can help! Shining Bright Tutors specializes in supporting elementary-aged students in a variety of areas including, but not limited, to Phonics, Reading, Writing, Spelling, Grammar, Math, Social Studies, and Science.

Middle School Tutoring

When children reach Middle School, the subjects start to get tougher. It can often be a challenge to support your child when they need it most. That is why we are here to support you. Shining Bright Tutors specializes in supporting middle school-aged students in a variety of areas including, but not limited, to Reading, Writing, Math, Social Studies, and Science.

High School Tutoring

High School is a time when students are determining the path they will take once they graduate. We are here to work with high school subjects such as English I-IV, Algebra 1 & 2, Geometry, Calculus, World History, etc. When you sign up, we can discuss what you are looking for so that we may help.

Test Prep

Standardized Testing is prevalent in our schools. This includes State Assessments and District Assessments such as the IOWA test, NWEA (MAP), SSAT, and ISEE. This even includes the SAT and ACT. We want to provide your child with what they need to be successful.
