    Where does tutoring take place?

    Tutoring sessions typically take place in a virtual setting. You will meet with your tutor either through Google Meet or Zoom. When you log in to your portal and check your calendar, you will be able to access the session link there.

  • Are you able
    tutor in
    person?

    Are you able to tutor in person?

    At this time, in person tutoring is only available in the Austin area. If you are in austin or surrounding areas, in person tutoring is limited and slots fill up quickly. All in person tutoring incurs an additional fee. Please contact us to see if we can accommodate your in person needs.

  • What do I
    need for

    What do I need for tutoring?

    Our platform is web based, therefore it works anywhere that has internet access. This includes on a phone, ipad/tablet, laptop, etc. Ideally a laptop would work best. You will also need a strong internet connection.
