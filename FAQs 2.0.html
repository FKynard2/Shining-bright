  • Where does

    Where does tutoring take place?

    Tutoring sessions typically take place in a virtual setting. You will meet with your tutor either through Google Meet or Zoom. When you log in to your portal and check your calendar, you will be able to access the session link there.

  • Are you able
    tutor in
    person?

    Are you able to tutor in person?

    At this time, in person tutoring is only available in the Austin area. If you are in austin or surrounding areas, in person tutoring is limited and slots fill up quickly. All in person tutoring incurs an additional fee. Please contact us to see if we can accommodate your in person needs.

  • What do I
    need for
    tutoring?

    What do I need for tutoring?

    Our platform is web based, therefore it works anywhere that has internet access. This includes on a phone, ipad/tablet, laptop, etc. Ideally a laptop would work best. You will also need a strong internet connection.

  • Am I able to
    check my
    child's
    progress?

    Am I able to check my child’s
    progress?

    As a parent, you will have access to the notes left by the tutor at the end of each session, which details your child's progress and performance.

  • What is your
    cancellation
    policy?

    What is your cancellation policy?

    Cancellation Policy for Students: If you cancel a tutoring session at least 24 hours before the scheduled time, you are eligible for a refund or rescheduling of the session. In case of a cancellation within 24 hours of the session, you will receive a refund of 50%, credited to your account. However, if you fail to attend the session without prior notice, there will be no refund.

    Cancellation Policy for Tutors: If a tutor cancels a session at least 24 hours before the scheduled time, you are eligible for rescheduling the session. In case of a cancellation within 24 hours of the session, you will receive one hour of free tutoring. If the tutor fails to show up for the session without prior notice, you are eligible for two hours of free tutoring.

  • Are payment
    plans
    available?

    Are payment plans available?

    The payment deadline is the date specified on the invoice, usually 24 hours before your first tutoring session. If you require a payment plan, please contact us, and we will assist you in setting it up.

    Please be aware that all payment plans will involve a monthly fee and require you to have a credit card on file with auto-pay enabled. Additionally, credit card payments will include a convenience fee of 4%.

  • Is there a
    sibling
    discount?

    Is there a sibling discount?

    Yes, a 5% discount will be provided for each sibling enrolled in our tutoring services.

  • Can my
    child receive
    help with
    their school
    work?

    Can my child receive help on
    schoolwork?

    Certainly! You can inform your tutor about the materials you plan to use during your session. You may upload and share the documents by scanning them and sending them to your tutor beforehand. This allows your tutor to be fully prepared for your lesson.

  • Is a diagnostic
    assessment
    required before
    tutoring?

    Is a diagnostic assessment required
    before tutoring?

    This is not mandatory. Your tutor will initiate contact to discuss your child's current performance and progress. Depending on your objectives, your tutor may conduct an informal assessment during the initial sessions.

  • Are 30-
    minute
    tutoring
    sessions
    available?

    Are 30-minute tutoring
    sessions available?

    Yes, we do offer 30-minute tutoring sessions to accommodate younger students, those who may have shorter attention spans, or difficulty sitting still for extended periods. However, it's important to note that more complex skills or topics may require additional time to fully cover and master.
